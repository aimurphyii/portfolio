About Me

Hey! I'm Aileen Murphy. I'm a Seattle native and a graduate of the University of Washington, class of 2009! I came out of UW with a bachelor's in Environmental Studies and went on to teach elementary science until I enlisted in the US Air Force where I served as a language and tactics analyst.

I've spent my whole career with my heart in service and software developement is no different. As a teacher my goal was to cultivate ecological literacy among my students and inspire future scientists and engineers. As an Airman, my duty was to provide timely and accurate intelligence for those downrange protecting our freedoms and our way of life. Their sacrifice allows us to create and innovate solutions for a better future, which is my focus as a software developer:

Making the world a better place, one app at a time.
